# Microsoft

Microsoft is a technology company that maintains several open-source repositories for AI/ML education and development, as well as frontier AI research models.

## Notable Repositories

- [MLforBeginners](https://github.com/microsoft/MLforBeginners) - Basic ML concepts in Jupyter notebooks, beginner-friendly and visual
- [AI Agents for Beginners](https://github.com/microsoft/ai-agents-for-beginners) - Free 11-lesson hands-on curriculum for AI agents
- [VibeVoice](https://github.com/microsoft/VibeVoice) - Open-source frontier voice AI (TTS + ASR) with 90-min long-form speech generation

## Focus Areas

- AI/ML education through open-source curricula
- Frontier voice AI research (VibeVoice)
- Beginner-friendly learning resources
- Practical, hands-on approach with Jupyter notebooks and lessons
