# Qwen

Qwen (通义千问) is a series of large language models and multimodal models developed by Alibaba Cloud's Tongyi Lab. The team has released various open-source models spanning LLMs, vision-language models, and speech generation.

## Key Releases

- **Qwen3-TTS**: Open-source TTS series (0.6B/1.7B) supporting voice clone, voice design, and 10 languages
- **Qwen LLMs**: Series of large language models for general-purpose AI tasks
- **Qwen-VL**: Vision-language models for multimodal understanding
