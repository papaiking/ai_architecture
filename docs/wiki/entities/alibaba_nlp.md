# Alibaba NLP (Tongyi Lab)

Alibaba's NLP research lab (Tongyi Lab), based in Hangzhou, Beijing, and Shanghai. Develops large language models and agentic AI systems for deep information-seeking tasks.

## Key Projects

- [DeepResearch](../sources/deepresearch.md) — Leading open-source deep research agent (18.9k stars)
- WebAgent project series — WebWalker, WebDancer, WebSailor, WebWatcher, WebResearcher, etc.
