# Daniel Miessler

Security researcher, writer, and creator of [PAI (Personal AI Infrastructure)](../sources/personal_ai_infrastructure.md) and [fabric](https://github.com/danielmiessler/fabric).

- **Website:** [danielmiessler.com](https://danielmiessler.com)
- **GitHub:** [github.com/danielmiessler](https://github.com/danielmiessler)
- **Twitter/X:** [@danielmiessler](https://twitter.com/danielmiessler)

## Key Projects

- **[PAI](../sources/personal_ai_infrastructure.md)** — Life Operating System built on Claude Code. Three-layer architecture (PAI OS, Pulse daemon, DA identity) with Algorithm v6.3.0, ISA primitive, 45 skills, 171 workflows, 37 hooks.
- **[fabric](https://github.com/danielmiessler/fabric)** — AI prompt patterns collection for specific tasks. Complementary to PAI.

## Vision

Wrote "The Real Internet of Things" (2016) predicting one DA per person as the primary interface to all AI. Core ideas:
1. Digital Assistants — one DA per person
2. Everything gets an API
3. Your DA dynamically creates your interfaces
4. You define your ideal state, AI helps you get there
