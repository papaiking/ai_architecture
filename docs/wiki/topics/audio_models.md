# Audio Models

Topic về các audio models, text-to-speech (TTS), speech-to-text (STT), và voice generation models.

## Text-to-Speech (TTS)

- [Qwen3-TTS](../sources/qwen3_tts.md) - Open-source TTS from Qwen (Alibaba) supporting voice clone, voice design, 10 languages, 97ms latency (11.1k stars)
- [VibeVoice](../sources/vibevoice.md) - Microsoft's frontier voice AI: 90-min multi-speaker TTS, 60-min ASR, 7.5Hz continuous tokenizers
- [Supertonic](../sources/supertonic.md) — Lightning-fast on-device multilingual TTS via ONNX Runtime, 99M params, 31 languages, 44.1kHz output, edge-device ready (7.7k stars)
