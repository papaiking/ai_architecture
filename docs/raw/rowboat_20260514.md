# Rowboat — Open-Source AI Coworker with Memory

- **Source:** https://github.com/rowboatlabs/rowboat
- **Author:** Rowboat Labs (Y Combinator S24)
- **Stars:** 14.1k
- **License:** Apache-2.0
- **Fetched:** May 14, 2026

---

Rowboat is an **open-source AI coworker** that connects to your email and meeting notes, builds a long-lived knowledge graph, and uses that context to help you get work done — privately, on your machine.

Capabilities:
- Build me a deck about our next quarter roadmap → generates PDF from knowledge graph
- Prep me for my meeting with Alex → pulls past decisions, open questions, relevant threads
- Track a person, company or topic through live notes
- Visualize, edit, and update knowledge graph anytime (plain Markdown)
- Record voice memos that auto-capture key takeaways

## How It's Different

Most AI tools reconstruct context on demand. Rowboat maintains **long-lived knowledge** instead:
- Context accumulates over time
- Relationships are explicit and inspectable
- Notes are editable by you (not hidden in a model)
- Everything lives on your machine as plain Markdown
- Obsidian-compatible vault with backlinks

## Integrations

- Gmail (email), Google Calendar, Rowboat meeting notes / Fireflies
- Composio.dev for product integrations
- MCP for external tools (Exa search, Twitter/X, ElevenLabs, Slack, Linear/Jira, GitHub)

## Install

Download for Mac/Windows/Linux: https://www.rowboatlabs.com/downloads

## Bring Your Own Model

- Local models via Ollama or LM Studio
- Hosted models (bring your API key)
- Swap anytime, data stays in local Markdown vault

## Features

- Meeting prep from prior decisions, threads, open questions
- Email drafting grounded in history and commitments
- Docs & decks generated from ongoing context (PDF slides)
- Follow-ups: capture decisions, action items, owners
- Live notes: auto-updating notes (@rowboat) that track competitors, topics, people
